What is POSH?

The Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) Act, 2013, mandates organizations to provide a safe working environment, especially for women. It ensures that any instance of sexual harassment is addressed promptly and appropriately, fostering a secure workplace culture.

Legal Framework
