Who We Are

At OHS, we are pioneers in promoting safe, inclusive, and legally compliant workplaces. Since our inception in 2015, we've empowered over 500 organizations to build environments that prioritize employee well-being and legal adherence through tailored POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) training, compliance audits, and expert consultancy services.

With a team of certified professionals, legal advisors, and training experts, we bring decades of combined experience in transforming organizational culture for the better. We're trusted by startups, corporates, and NGOs alike to deliver results that matter.
