Understanding POSH Compliance

The Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) Act is a cornerstone legislation that protects employees against workplace harassment. It mandates the establishment of an Internal Committee (IC) in every organization with 10 or more employees.

Beyond legal compliance, organizations must foster a culture of respect, equality, and safety. This involves regular training, awareness programs, and strong policy enforcement.
