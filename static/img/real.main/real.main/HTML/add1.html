  • What is the role of the ICC in a company?
    The Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) handles POSH complaints and ensures a fair inquiry process.
  • Is POSH training mandatory for all employees?
    Yes, all employees must undergo awareness training annually as per legal guidelines.
  • What are the penalties for non-compliance?
    Organizations may face fines up to ‚Çπ50,000, cancellation of licenses, and reputational damage.

  • How can I become a certified POSH trainer?
    You must attend certified POSH training programs and register with appropriate bodies.
  • What content should a training program include?
    Legal background, case studies, role plays, reporting procedures, and ICC responsibilities.
  • How often should refresher training be conducted?
    At least once every year to stay updated with legal changes and practical scenarios.

  • What counts as sexual harassment under the POSH Act?
    Unwelcome behavior including physical contact, comments, gestures, or requests for favors.
  • How do I file a complaint?
    Submit a written complaint to your organization's ICC within 3 months of the incident.
  • Can complaints be filed anonymously?
    No. The law requires the complainant to identify themselves, though confidentiality is maintained throughout the process.
