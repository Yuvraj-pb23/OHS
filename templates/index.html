End-to-End POSH Compliance

Comprehensive Organizational Compliance

Achieving compliance with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013, is more than just a checkbox exercise; it is about mitigating risk and building a safe culture.

What We Deliver:
  • Audit & Assessment: A complete health check of workplace safety.
  • IC Constitution: Setup of Internal Committees at all units.
  • Statutory Reporting: Drafting Annual Reports for the District Officer.
  • Mandatory Training: Awareness sessions and skill-building workshops.
  • Documentation: Templates for posters, notices, and minutes.
Outcome: A 100% compliant, audit-ready organization.

POSH Policy Formulation

Drafting Robust & Customised Policies

A well-drafted policy is the backbone of a safe workplace. We move beyond generic templates to create policies that reflect your specific organizational structure, industry type, and cultural values.

Key Elements:
  • Industry Adaptation: Policies tailored for IT, Manufacturing, or Remote work.
  • Gender Neutrality: Options to protect all employees while maintaining legal safeguards.
  • Redressal Mechanism: Clearly defined steps for filing complaints and inquiries.
  • Zero Tolerance: Articulating exactly what constitutes sexual harassment.
Outcome: A clear, legally sound document employees trust.

She-Box & Case Handling

Expert Case Management

Handling a sexual harassment complaint requires extreme sensitivity, confidentiality, and adherence to natural justice. We guide you through both internal complaints and those filed via the government SHe-Box portal.

Our Support Includes:
  • SHe-Box Navigation: Registering and responding to complaints on the portal.
  • Inquiry Procedures: Step-by-step guidance on interviewing parties.
  • Evidence Management: Professional handling of digital and physical evidence.
  • Conciliation: Handling settlement requests legally.
Outcome: Fair investigations that stand legal scrutiny.

Legal Support & Query Desk

On-Demand Legal Expertise

POSH cases often involve complex legal gray areas. Our dedicated legal desk provides real-time support to HR heads and IC members to resolve queries and ensure procedural correctness.

Services Offered:
  • Advisory Services: Immediate answers to procedural questions.
  • Document Vetting: Legal review of notices and reports.
  • Appeals & Litigation: Guidance if a party challenges the decision.
  • Consequence Management: Helping implement disciplinary actions lawfully.
Outcome: Decisions backed by legal experts.

External Member Support

Impartial Third-Party Expertise

The POSH Act mandates the inclusion of an External Member in the Internal Committee to ensure neutrality. We provide qualified, experienced professionals to serve on your panel.

Role of Our Members:
  • Unbiased Perspective: Ensuring no internal politics influence the investigation.
  • Process Adherence: Acting as the guardian of the law.
  • Mentorship: Hand-holding internal IC members.
  • Experience: Insights from handling 200+ cases.
Outcome: A balanced, fair committee.

POCSO Compliance

Protection of Children from Sexual Offences

For organizations dealing with minors (schools, sports academies, NGOs), compliance with the POCSO Act, 2012 is mandatory. We help create a safe environment for children by implementing strict child protection protocols and sensitization programs.

Our POCSO Services:
  • Child Protection Policy: Drafting specific policies for interacting with minors.
  • Staff Sensitization: Training teachers and support staff on appropriate behavior.
  • Mandatory Reporting: Educating staff on the legal obligation to report offenses.
  • Student Workshops: Age-appropriate 'Good Touch, Bad Touch' awareness sessions.
Outcome: A secure environment where children are protected and staff are empowered.
