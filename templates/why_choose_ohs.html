A Holistic Approach

End-to-End POSH Compliance

In today’s evolving workplace environment, compliance with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 is not merely a statutory requirement—it is a commitment to dignity, safety, and trust at work. True compliance goes beyond having a policy in place; it requires a structured, sustained, and sensitive approach that addresses prevention, redressal, and accountability at every level.

Open Hand Solutions is a consulting company committed to providing end-to-end POSH compliance services, ensuring that organizations are fully aligned with both the letter and spirit of the law. Our approach is comprehensive, practical, and legally sound, enabling organizations to create workplaces that are not only compliant but genuinely safe and inclusive.

1. Internal Committee (IC) Setup & Constitution

We assist organizations in the proper constitution of the Internal Committee in accordance with the POSH Act, including guidance on composition, eligibility, tenure, roles, and responsibilities. This ensures the IC is legally valid, functional, and equipped to discharge its duties effectively.

2. Training of Internal Committee Members

We conduct structured and in-depth training programs for IC members covering legal provisions, inquiry procedures, principles of natural justice, report writing, and case handling. Our trainings are designed to build confidence, neutrality, and procedural clarity among committee members.

3. Employee Sensitisation & Awareness Programs

We deliver employee sensitisation sessions across all levels of the organization to foster awareness about acceptable workplace behavior, rights and responsibilities, reporting mechanisms, and the importance of a respectful work culture.

4. Awareness Through Posters & IEC Material

Open Hand Solutions designs and provides POSH-compliant posters and Information, Education and Communication (IEC) materials to ensure continuous visibility and awareness within the workplace, as mandated under the Act.

5. Safety & Workplace Compliance Support

We support organizations in aligning workplace practices with broader safety and dignity-related compliance requirements, reinforcing preventive measures and organizational accountability.

6. Case Management & Legal Desk Support

Our dedicated case and legal desk provides handholding support during complaint handling, from receipt of complaints to inquiry management and documentation, ensuring due process and confidentiality at every stage.

7. External Member Support

We provide qualified and experienced External Members to Internal Committees, ensuring independence, legal insight, and credibility in the inquiry process, as required under the POSH Act.

8. She-Box Registration & Compliance

We assist organizations with registration on the Government’s She-Box portal and ensure ongoing compliance with reporting and procedural requirements linked to the platform.

A Practical and Engaging Approach

Our Training Methodology

POSH training is most effective when employees do more than just listen—they must understand, relate, and apply the learning in real workplace situations. At Open Hand Solutions, we follow a practical and engaging training approach that moves beyond conventional lectures to create meaningful learning experiences.

1. Structured PPTs

We use well-designed, easy-to-understand presentations that clearly explain key legal provisions, workplace expectations, reporting mechanisms, and roles and responsibilities. Content is adapted to suit the audience and avoids unnecessary legal jargon.

2. Impactful Videos

Short, impactful videos are used to demonstrate workplace scenarios, behavioural nuances, and real-life situations. Visual storytelling helps participants relate to concepts and understand the impact of actions and inactions.

Role plays allow participants to step into real workplace situations—helping them understand perspectives, appropriate responses, and the importance of timely reporting and intervention.

5. Group Games

Group activities and games promote open discussion, teamwork, and shared learning. They help break inhibitions, encourage participation, and make difficult topics easier to discuss in a safe and structured environment.

6. Case Discussions

We use practical case examples to help participants understand how POSH issues arise, how they are addressed, and what best practices look like. This ensures clarity on both prevention and redressal mechanisms.

Embedding Confidentiality and Sensitivity

Confidentiality is the backbone of an effective POSH framework. Without it, trust erodes, reporting declines, and the integrity of the inquiry process is compromised. At Open Hand Solutions (OHS), confidentiality and sensitive handling are not assumed skills—they are actively taught, reinforced, and monitored at every stage of POSH compliance.

Training IC Members

Confidentiality as a Core Training Module

OHS training programs for IC members place strong emphasis on:
• Statutory confidentiality obligations under the POSH Act
• Consequences of breach—legal, reputational, and ethical
• Practical dos and don’ts in communication, documentation, and discussion
This ensures IC members are clear that confidentiality is not optional—it is mandatory.

Practical Scenarios and Risk Identification

Through case-based discussions and scenario exercises, IC members are trained to identify:
• High-risk points where information leakage commonly occurs
• Informal channels that must be avoided
• Behavioural lapses such as casual discussions, unnecessary emails, or unsecured record sharing

Ensuring Confidentiality

Controlled Inquiry Execution

During active inquiries, OHS ensures that:
• Information is shared strictly on a need-to-know basis
• Interviews are conducted discreetly and respectfully
• Statements, evidence, and inquiry notes are handled with controlled access

External Member Support

As External Members, OHS professionals bring independence, neutrality, and strong ethical standards. Information accessed during inquiries is:
• Used strictly for inquiry purposes
• Never discussed outside the committee
• Not shared internally or externally beyond statutory requirements

Beyond the Workplace

POCSO Compliance & Child Safety

Safety and dignity are fundamental rights that extend beyond corporate offices to educational institutions, NGOs, and child-care hubs. The Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012, mandates strict safety protocols for any organization dealing with minors.

Open Hand Solutions provides specialized consulting to help schools, sports complexes, and social organizations implement robust child protection frameworks that are sensitive, legally compliant, and vigilant.

1. Child Protection Policy (CPP)

We draft customized Child Protection Policies and Codes of Conduct for staff, ensuring they align with POCSO guidelines and institutional values. This includes protocols for physical contact, digital safety, and behavioral boundaries.

2. Staff & Caregiver Sensitization

Specialized training for teachers, administrative staff, support staff (drivers, guards), and volunteers on identifying signs of abuse, mandatory reporting obligations, and maintaining a high standard of professional ethics.

3. Child Protection Committee (CPC) Setup

We assist in the formation and training of Internal Child Protection Committees. We define their roles in grievance handling, monitoring safety standards, and ensuring that any concerns are addressed with immediate priority and sensitivity.

4. Safety Audits & Compliance Mapping

Comprehensive audits of institutional premises, transport systems, and recruitment processes (background checks) to identify vulnerability points and ensure the environment is 100% safe for children.

OHS ensures that POCSO compliance is handled with zero-tolerance for lapses and absolute priority on child safety.
